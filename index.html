Manchester-based artist, musician, and multipotentialite with a passion for geek culture, creativity, and connection. I’m currently diving into coding, blending my design expertise with technical skills to build accessible, user-friendly experiences that look great and work seamlessly across devices. Always learning, always creating, and always ready for the next adventure.
